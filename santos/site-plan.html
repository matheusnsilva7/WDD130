Content

Home page

Welcome to our team homepage, we hope to increase your knowledge and love for our team.

through this site we hope you can learn more about football and have fun with friends, Football (also called football especially in other countries) is the most popular sport in the world and it is played in most countries, so we also hope that you can connect with people from all over the world and celebrate with them our victories.

the home page will have several sections that will help us to achieve this goal, the first section will have the theme of our time and the next 2 games our team will play, the next sections will be about the history of the team, the trophies and the player who made it history in our team.

the last section before the footer will have a place where we will put the news about the team and the user will be able to see what is happening with the team and if there are any changes.

the home page will be perfect both for those who are fans and know everything about the team and for those who do not know much about the team.

Team

Welcome to our team page.

soccer is a team sport, involving 11 players on each side who use their legs, head and torso to pass the ball and score goals. As a team activity can be a powerful way to build collaboration and trust, improve motivation, nurture strengths and address weaknesses.

this page will be perfect for you to learn more about our players, each player will have information about him on the page like name, age, position and a little more information about himself.

this informational page is how fans will connect with players and learn more about them.

we will also have a section with players who made history in our team and information about them.

before the footer there will be a place where we will put the specific news about the team in case someone is injured or a new player in the team.

schedule

Welcome to the schedule page, here is were you can see the result of the previous game and are able to see the nexts games that our team is gonna play.

in each game is gonna have information like where is gonna be and the time of the game and if it is a previous game is gonna have who made the goal.

the championship is based on points, the champion is the club that adds the most points at the end of the 38 rounds of dispute, each victory is worth three points, each draw is worth one point and each loss is not scored. If two or more times they finish the running points phase with the same number of points, the tiebreaker is made by tiebreaker criteria.

before the footer there will be a place where we will put the specific news about the game that we are gonna play and what the fans can spect of our time in the games.
